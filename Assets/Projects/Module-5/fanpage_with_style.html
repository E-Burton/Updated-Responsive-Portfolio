Biography

Jamie Foxx is a mutlifaceted artist and considered one of the greatest artists of his generation. He started out his career in the 1990's as a comedian, later transitioning into acting, all the while showcasing his singing ability. He played the R&B musician Ray Charles, in the movie 'Ray' in 2004, winning Best Actor at the Academy Awards and Golden Globes. In the midst of acting and standup, he also released several albums with songs reaching the top of the Pop, R&B, and Hip-Hop charts on various occassions.

Jamie Foxx Fun Facts

#1   Jamie Foxx's name at birth was Eric Marlon Bishop.
#2   He came up with the stage name Jamie Foxx during his open mic stand-up days at comdey clubs. He choose the name because it sounded ambiguous and also as a tribute to comedian Redd Foxx.
#3   He is the second male in history, who has received the best actor Oscar nomination for two different movies (Ray and Collateral), in the same year.
